About me

My life rule is never stop learning and always look for a new way of developing myself. I have a wide experience in a different fields. I was born in a little snow city in Russia.City is a loud word for it was: just a single apartment building located ina border with China. When I was 5y.o. we moved closer to the center of the country a little city Penza where I got graduated first from high school, then State University with a bachelor degree in management and economics. During university education I have started my work experience and got practice as a warehouse operator, delivery coordinator and executive assistant to Head of Sales.
My life got significantly changed in 2019 when I moved to the USA and started a new life there. My family and I moved from Seattle (Washington) to Los Angeles (California) and now we are staying in Fort Worth(Texas). Despite the fact that the move is exhausting and difficult I find it as an enjoying challenge. I love new tasks and environment, adapting to them pretty quickly, focusing on advantages of new place and improving disadvantages (when possible).
Right now I am working as a fulfillment associate at Amazon facility in Haslet(Texas) and my responsibilities include stowing products, working and fixing the robotic drives, managing floor health.
I love my work and the company. Its important for me to follow company's values and goals and find a ways to improve myself as a worker and as a person.
